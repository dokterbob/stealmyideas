* Organic 'wastes'

  * Sawdust

  * Paper

  * Low-grade chemical waste
  * (Hu)manure
* Atmospheric carbon (carbon credits)

* Honey
* Compost
* Wood
* Nuts
* Fruits
* Bio-fertilizer
* Biodiesel
* Methane
* Fish

* Fermentation
* Carbon sequestation

* Aquaculture
* Accounting

## Maximize
